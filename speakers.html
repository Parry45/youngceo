Meet the Thought Leader

Mr Theodore Albright

“Theodore is a Lawyer ; Thought Leader; Strategist and the Managing Consultant of The Family Office Africa, a multi disciplinary consulting firm .Theodore holds an LLB degree, MBA from University of Manchester. He is also a qualified Certified Trust and Estate Practitioner with a Postgraduate Diploma in Risk and Compliance via the International Compliance Association. Theodore currently acts as Advisor to numerous boards. He has been engaged in training over a thousand leaders including hundreds of CEOs across Africa. He is also a Fellow of the Institute of Directors, Ghana and a member of the Institute of Directors in England”
