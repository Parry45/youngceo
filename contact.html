Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

  • What is the Young CEO Dialogues Series?
    The Young CEO Dialogues Series is a leadership and networking event designed to empower and connect emerging business leaders. The 2024 edition, NextGen Go Pro, focuses on equipping young CEOs with the tools, insights, and strategies needed to accelerate their growth and success.

  • Who should attend?
    This event is ideal for aspiring and established young CEOs, entrepreneurs, and senior executives looking to enhance their leadership skills, expand their professional network, and explore innovative business strategies.

  • When and where will the event take place?
    The event is scheduled to take place over two days at [Alisa Hotel], on the 14th and 15th of December, 2024.

  • What can attendees expect?
    Participants will engage in interactive workshops, insightful keynote sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities with industry experts and fellow leaders.

  • How can I register?
    You can register for the event right here on this website at the “Register Now” Option or contact us at [024 130 4050] for more details.
